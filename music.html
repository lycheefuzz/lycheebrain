I do be dabbling in the music, though. Music has been a huge part of my life. For most of my life, I've played instruments. As the lifestory of most Asian Americans, it started with the piano. Which then moved to the guitar, and then my band kid phase hit, and I went to clarinet. Then, my emo tumblr phase hit and I picked up the ukulele. And then my true punk kid phase hit, and I picked up the drums. Then, my band kid side was fueled again, and then I was in my high school's drumline for 4 years, and played drums for our jazz choir/jazz band for 3 years. I was also in a band for 5 years with one of my best friends during all of this, and made an album with her! Which will obvi get its own little segment here.


Is this me writing my music journey as a way of being like "Why am I a STEM student, why didn't I pursue music?". Maybe. My answer is this: job security. (Plus, music is forever! I can still do music.)
